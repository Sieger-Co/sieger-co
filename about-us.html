Sieger & Co is an information technology company operating out of Lagos, Nigeria that specializes in the development of bespoke unique novel cutting-edge solutions to the challenges of oil and gas companies.

Sieger provides solutions across various computing disciplines to clients depending on their unique requirements. We pride ourselves on being at the cutting edge of computing innovation and wherever possible, we strive to apply the latest technologies to our development work in order to deliver the benefits of those breakthroughs to our clients.

We have developed and deployed multiple software solutions for various energy companies.

Our Services.

Here are some of the services we provide.

  • 1

    Desktop Application Development
    We design and develop custom applications for Windows, MacOS and Linux-based operating systems as well as for the web.

    Whether you're looking to build highly technical engineering software, or applications that simply improve your operational efficiency, our engineers and software developers will help you every step of the way, from solution design to delivery and ongoing maintenance.

    See an Example

  • 2

    Artificial Intelligence
    We apply Artificial Intelligence (AI) towards simplifying operations and solving various technical and operational problems.

    Our data engineers create and deploy machine learning models to aid in data analytics for decision making and problem solving. From reinforcement learning, to machine vision and deep learning Sieger uses AI to solve oil and gas problems.

    See an Example

  • 3

    Mobile Application Development
    We develop Android and iOS mobile applications for energy companies. We handle all aspects of app development from business analysis and UI/UX design to application testing, deployment and on-going support.

    Our mobile applications fulfill myriad functions such as providing dashboards and reports on the operational status of an asset to stakeholders, incident management and reporting, and calculations and/or data retrieval among others.

    See an Example

  • 4

    Cloud Computing
    We develop and deploy Microsoft Azure, AWS and GCP cloud solutions for oil and gas companies. These solutions can for example allow complex, time-consuming calculations to be carried out on an offsite server, eliminating the need for setting up or managing in-house web and application servers, and allowing such calculations be carried out on any device, regardless of the computing power available on the device itself.

    Our cloud solutions can be deployed to either public/shared cloud, private cloud or some mix of both options, depending on client and regulatory requirements.

    See an Example

  • 5

    Data Analytics & Big Data
    We help oil and gas companies gain more actionable intelligence from the data generated by their business operations.

    We typically do this by designing Dashboards and Reports using Tableau and Power BI, or creating custom scripts and visualizations. We also deploy databases, data warehouses and cloud data services if required.

  • 6

    Robotics
    We deploy UAVs and Aerial Drones for automated or manual asset monitoring.

    Our drones can be set to automatically surveil an area intermittently – triggered either by a timer, a human operator, or an unexpected loss of fluid pressure (if real time pressure or volume monitoring is available on an asset).

    See an Example

How we work.

1
First Contact
Upon first contact from you, we aim to get back to you in less than 24 hours.

We will request more details about your project if necessary, and setup a follow up meeting via video conference at a time that works for you to discuss the project further.
2
Requirements Gathering
At this stage, a few members of our team will begin discussions with you to learn about and understand the full scope of your project.

These discussions will help us determine what technologies and solutions best suit your requirements and at the end of the requirements gathering process, our team and yours will know the technologies, timelines and cost implications for your project.

If your proposed project will be research intensive (as is typical for cutting-edge AI related projects), we also carry out feasibility studies at this stage.
3
Design & Development
Once all the details of the project have been agreed upon, we will begin the product design process for your project working closely with members of your team.

Once the project design is adopted, we will begin software development. We believe an agile approach to software development generally works best and we will continue to develop parts of your project and demo them to you for review on a weekly or bi-weekly basis. This approach allows for increased collaboration and alignment between our team and yours.

We may also use a classic (plan-driven) waterfall development methodology (in place of an agile one) if your project has a clearly defined scope, and there is little or no risk of scope creep.
4
Delivery & Support
Once the project is completed (as determined by both our team and yours), we deliver the developed software, a user manual, technical and design documentation and the source code to you.

Unless otherwise agreed at the requirements gathering stage, you retain all intellectual property rights to your project.

We also provide maintenance and support services for continuous improvement software improvement.
